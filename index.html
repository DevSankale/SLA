SLA FOUNDATION.

An organization with a youth focus that promotes social and economic development, peace, and unity through advocacy, psychosocial support, community outreach, networking, and support for education.

Transforming lives one step at a time.

Provision of sustainable and profitable means of livelihoods, training of Income Generating Activities and financial management, support to Orphans and Vulnerable Children to attain vocational skills and provision of basic start up tool kits to beneficiaries.
